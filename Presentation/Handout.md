# Handout: Linky Quick Facts

**Project Name:** Linky | Symmetric P2P Share  
**Version:** 2.0 Stable  
**Website:** [index.html (Local)]  

---

### 🌟 Key Features
*   **Direct P2P:** No middleman, no servers, just speed.
*   **Symmetric:** Bidirectional sharing—send and receive at once.
*   **Encrypted:** AES-GCM 256 + SHA-256 Hashing.
*   **Instant:** No accounts or sign-ups required.
*   **Beautiful:** Modern glassmorphic UI with dynamic mesh backgrounds.

### 🛡️ Privacy Profile
*   **Zero Logs:** We don't track what you send.
*   **Zero Storage:** Your files never touch a disk on the internet.
*   **Ephemeral:** Everything is deleted when you close the tab.

### 🚀 How to use
1.  Open **Linky**.
2.  **Host** a room or **Join** with a PIN.
3.  **Drop** files and watch them beam!
