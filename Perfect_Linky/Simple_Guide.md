# Linky: A Simple Guide for Everyone

## 🌟 What is Linky?
Imagine you want to give a physical photo to a friend. You walk over and hand it to them directly. There's no middleman, and nobody else sees the photo.

**Linky is the digital version of that.** 

Most apps (like Google Drive or WhatsApp) make you "upload" your file to their computer (a server) first, and then your friend "downloads" it from them. Linky skips that middle step. It creates a private, invisible tunnel directly between your device and your friend's device.

## 🚀 Why use Linky?
1. **Total Privacy:** Since the file never touches another computer, nobody—not even the creators of Linky—can see what you're sharing.
2. **Super Fast:** It's like a straight line between two points. No waiting for uploads to finish before the download starts.
3. **No Mess:** You don't need to create an account, remember a password, or "delete" the file later. Once you close the tab, it's gone.
4. **It's Free & Simple:** Just a PIN code, like a secret handshake.

## 🛠️ How do I use it? (The 3-Step Dance)
1. **The Host (Sender):** One person clicks "HOST" and gets a secret PIN (like `SUMMER-2026`).
2. **The Guest (Receiver):** The other person types that PIN into their screen.
3. **The Share:** Once you see the "Link Established" green light, just drag your files into the box. They "beam" over instantly!

## 💡 Fun Fact
Linky is "Symmetric." That's a fancy word meaning once you're connected, both people can send and receive at the same time. It's a two-way street!
